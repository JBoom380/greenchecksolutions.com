1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us. This includes:

  • Contact form submissions, which may include your name, email address, company name, and the content of your message
  • Usage data collected through standard server logs, such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit on this site

We do not collect payment information, and we do not create user accounts.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and communicate with you about our consulting services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Server log data is used to understand aggregate site usage and to diagnose technical issues. This data is not used to identify individual visitors.

3. Data Storage

Contact form submissions on this site are processed and stored by Formspree. We do not maintain our own database of form submissions or store personal data on our own servers. For information on how Formspree handles your data, please refer to Formspree's privacy policy.

4. Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. The only browser storage used is localStorage to remember your dark mode preference between visits. This preference is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

This site makes use of two third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — Typography is loaded from Google's font CDN. Google may collect anonymized request data as described in their privacy policy.
  • Formspree — Our contact form is processed by Formspree. Submissions are subject to Formspree's terms and privacy policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other behavioral tracking or advertising technology.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data you submitted through our contact form at any time. To make a deletion request, email us at info@greenchecksolutions.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will forward your request to Formspree and confirm once deletion is complete.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please reach out:

  • Email: info@greenchecksolutions.com
  • Green Check Solutions LLC, Idaho, USA
